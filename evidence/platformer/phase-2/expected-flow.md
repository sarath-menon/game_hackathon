# Expected Flow: Side-Scrolling Platformer Phase 2 Second Retest

The recording should show Skyline Stepper loading at the title/start screen, then starting the run. The HUD should show 3 health, 0/8 glow cores, and distance to the exit.

The recorded route should use the documented lower-deck safe path, move right through the side-scrolling level, collect at least one visible blue glow core, preserve jump/gravity/platform/camera behavior, and reach the green EXIT beacon.

The recording should end on `Level Complete` with a summary that reports collected glow cores and remaining health. Separate black-box probes should confirm red vent contact still damages and respawns the player, and repeated damage can produce the health-zero `Run Ended` summary.
