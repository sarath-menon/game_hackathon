# Cookie Clicker Player Manual

Source URL: `https://orteil.dashnet.org/cookieclicker/`

## Objective

Click the large cookie to produce cookies, buy upgrades and buildings, and grow cookie production over time.

## Controls

- Click the large cookie to gain cookies.
- Click available products, buildings, and upgrades when you have enough cookies.
- Use the Options menu for game settings, saving, export/import, language, and reset-related controls if available.
- Do not reset progress unless the reset behavior is the specific thing being tested.

## Player-Facing Behaviors To Expect

- Cookie count and production rate should update after clicks or purchases.
- Purchases should reduce cookies and increase production or unlock visible progression.
- The Options menu should expose save/export/import or other configuration behavior.
- Local browser storage may preserve progress between reloads.
