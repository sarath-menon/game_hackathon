    Ash Circuit

    Defeat the Glass Warden with a deterministic starter deck. Each player turn starts with 3 energy and a five-card hand. The enemy intent panel shows its next move before you end your turn.

    After the first combat, choose one reward card. The selected card is added to your deck before the second encounter. Win the second encounter for final victory.
